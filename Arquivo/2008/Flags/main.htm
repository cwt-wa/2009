If you just stumbled upon this site you have entered the demented world of the Crespo's Worms Tournament. I'm Crespo, the host. On 2002 I decided to create a tournament for normal wormers due to the fact that Wormnet 2 didn't show the quality and number of good normal wormers that I imagined it should. It gave me a lot of hard work but the result was very satisfactory because now I have the safety to say that this tournament is probably one of the last places on Wormnet 2 where you can find yourself decent normal games against honourable wormers and by now (2006) CWT has been used as a model for other comunities or tournaments relating normal gameplay.

This tournament is my pride and joy and it gives me an enourmous sense of well-being to be the host of such a nice comunity of wormers. I think the rules are now improved to what is a totaly straight way to compete and the fact that Worms is a social game allows us to go beyond playing Worms. In fact, many of us often use MSN Messenger to make chats and talk about many things other than Worms or the tournament itself, which allows us to establish some rather unusual friendships.

Welcome everyone, since Crespo has retired from worms We (Khamski, Jigsaw, Koras, Antares, Joschi and some other great fellas) decided to carry on with this great annual tournament.
The tourney starts on October 15th,keep an eye on forum for upcoming news.
